WELCOME TO ROCK, PAPER, SCISSORS!

The objective of the game is to beat the computor at Rock,Paper,Scissors.
The users results are on the left, and the computors are on the right.
The user and the computor will recieve a random generated ("Rock", "Paper", "Scissors)
Select play to start and select play to continue trying your luck against the machine!
The user starts of with 0 points - each game won results in 50 points for the user.
50 points are deducted each time you loose.
